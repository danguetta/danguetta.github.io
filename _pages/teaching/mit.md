While at MIT, I was fortunate to work as a Graduate Teaching Assistant for courses 8.01 (Classical Mechanics) and 8.02 (Electromagnetism). This page contains the materials I created for these purposes

## 8.01 - Classical Mechanics

* Review sessions
    - [Handout](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/noSlip.pdf) explaining the no-slip condition for a rolling object
    - A [formula sheet](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/formulaSheet.pdf) summarizing many of the equations of classical mechanics
    - A [very long handout](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/ContMassTransfer.pdf) explaining how to deal with mechanics problems involving continuous mass transfer
    - A [presentation](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/SHMPresentation.pps) on simple harmonic motion
* Practice problems
    - [67 pages](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/lotsOfProbs.pdf) worth of more mechanics questions you could ever solve. All have answers, some have solutions
    - [Questions](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/rotational_review_questions.pdf) and [solutions](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/rotational_review_solutions.pdf) on a review session on rotational motion (including gyroscopes)
    - Quizzes with solutions: 2 ([questions](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/Quiz2.pdf), [solutions](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/Quiz2Sol.pdf)), 3 ([questions](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/Quiz3.pdf), [solutions](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/Quiz3Sol.pdf)), 4 ([questions](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/Quiz4.pdf), [solutions](/files/teaching/mit_physics/8_01/Quiz4Sol.pdf))
